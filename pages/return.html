Refund Policy

We aim to deliver high-quality books to our customers. However, in rare cases where the product is damaged, we provide a return, replacement, or refund option. This policy ensures fairness for both the customer and our store.

Our return policy is strictly valid for 3 days from the date of delivery. After this period, we will not be able to process any return or refund request under any circumstances.

Unboxing Requirement

Customers must record a clear unboxing video while opening the package. This video acts as proof in case of any damage or issue with the product. Without a proper unboxing video, no return or refund request will be accepted.

Conditions for Return

  • Request must be raised within 3 days of delivery
  • Product should be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging must be available
  • Unboxing video must clearly show the issue

Eligible Cases

  • Severe spine damage where pages are coming out
  • Major damage during transit
  • More than 2 pages misprinted or missing
  • Wrong item delivered

Non-Eligible Cases

  • Minor scratches, dents, or corner bends
  • Damage caused after delivery
  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Request made after 3 days

Refund Process

Once your return request is approved, the refund will be initiated within 5–7 working days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. In case of replacement, a new product will be shipped to your address.

Important Note

We reserve the right to reject any return request if the provided information is incorrect or if the product does not meet our return policy criteria. Please read all terms carefully before submitting a request.
